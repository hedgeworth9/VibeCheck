With Vibe Check, you can compare and contrast different units based on earthquake safety, price, convenience!
Having trouble with spotting red flags?
Are you planning to purchase or lease a property but you're not sure if the structure is safe enough to withstand disasters like earthquakes? If so, then Vibe Check is for you!
What exactly is Vibe Check?
Vibe Check is an online tool specifically designed to help consumers assess whether or not a property meets the minimum requirements to be considered earthquake-resistant and safe to inhabit.

It guides users to ask the right questions with regards to safety and provides an efficient way to narrow down the best property options at a glance.
Why bother spotting red flags before making a purchase?
All businesses should practice mandatory due diligence which is a legal requirement that ensures no harm will befall their customers who avail of their products and services. Unfortunately, there are instances when businesses do not practice mandatory due diligence. This is why consumers need to know how to spot red flags and protect themselves from establishments that fail to take legally-required safety measures against risks such as safety hazards.
